Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

Introduction

At CicloBeef Studio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, interact with our services, or communicate with us through social media platforms.

Our project specializes in offering high-quality artistic products with a historical, social, economic, and political theme. To provide the best possible service, we employ advanced automation tools and integrate multiple communication channels.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services effectively and securely. Types of information collected include:

  • Personal Identification Information: such as your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Usage Data: information on how you interact with our website and communications (e.g., IP address, browser type, referring URLs).
  • Communication Data: messages exchanged via social media channels (Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn), emails, and automated bots.
  • Transaction Data: purchase history, payment confirmations, and order details.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the purposes of providing, managing, and improving our services:

  • To process orders and deliver products accurately and timely.
  • To automate communication flows using AI tools such as LangChain and LangGraph, ensuring professional and prompt customer service.
  • To manage payment processing securely via PayPal and related financial services.
  • To send you transactional emails, order confirmations, and follow-ups including digital content such as PDFs related to your purchase.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or abuse.
  • To analyze website usage to optimize user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and will never sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with trusted third parties strictly for the purposes of:

  • Processing payments through authorized payment gateways (e.g., PayPal, Wise).
  • Order fulfillment through our internal distributor, WhiteWall, under strict confidentiality and data protection measures.
  • Compliance with legal requests, law enforcement, or regulations when required by law.
  • Service providers helping us automate and manage communications (e.g., Google Apps Script for emails, n8n for workflow automation).

Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our systems are continuously monitored and updated to meet security best practices, including the use of secure hosting platforms, encryption, and secure API access.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the US, Europe, and UAE), you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Request data portability or copies of your information.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at ciclobeefstudio@gmail.com.
