# Our Team

Ciclobeef Studio brings together professionals in art, design, research and technology.  
Our combined experience allows us to approach each piece as both an artistic and an intellectual project.

---

## Core Team (Representative Roles)

**Art Direction & Curation**  
Leads the artistic concept, visual coherence and narrative direction of each piece.

**Historical & Social Research**  
Ensures that every work is grounded in reliable sources, contextual analysis and ethical considerations.

**Visual Design & Production**  
Transforms concepts and research into finished visual pieces ready for high-end printing and framing.

**Technology & Automation**  
Designs and maintains the technical foundation for digital presence, documentation, APIs and future automation with Meta Graph API and other platforms.

---

## How We Work

- We treat each project as a collaboration between art, research and technology.  
- We document our process and decisions to maintain transparency and reproducibility.  
- We design with international standards in mind, aiming for long-term relevance and institutional compatibility.
